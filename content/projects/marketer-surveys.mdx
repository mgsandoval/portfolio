## The problem

{/* What was broken or missing before this app existed? */}

The marketing team needed an app where they could create, edit, and respond to surveys, and view the 
results, without having to use Google Forms.

## What I built

{/* Your role, the architecture, role/permission model, anything tricky. */}

I built an app where users could take surveys, and later added the ability to create surveys with 
sections and questions featuring different response types, such as single-choice, multiple-choice, scales ranging from X to Y, or 
text responses.

It includes a highly customizable survey builder that allows users to drag and drop questions to reorder them, making it 
easy to create surveys.
