## The problem

{/* What was broken or missing before this dashboard existed? */}

Difiesta wanted to look at its month-closures finances by company branch for comparing sales results of the past 2 months, but there 
was no way  to see the sales results without a query to the database. Also, they wanted to see the results of each Honduras
department.


## What I built

{/* Your role, the architecture, the map/filtering implementation. */}
As the Difiesta's software developer, I made a dashboard web app where the users of the company can log in and see the results
of the company by branch. They can filter by month to check the results of each branch.

I built a map dashboard where the company can filter and select the Honduras map to check sales by department and by municipality.


## Outcome

{/* Impact: who uses it, what decisions it supports, etc. */}
The company checks the sales results at the end of the month, they check results looking at the dashboard by company branch, 
and they compare sales results by Honduras departments and municipalities.
